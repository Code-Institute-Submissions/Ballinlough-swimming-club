Membership

Ballinlough Swimming Club is Ireland's newest swimming club at just 6 months old. We are always looking for new members. Although we are run entirely by volunteers, we incur expenses such as insurance and equipment costs.

Type Price Description
Full €100.00 Admittance to all training sessions, use of club equipment, subsidised club outings and entry to some competitions
Student €80.00 Reduced full membership rate for all full-time third level students.
Unemployed €75.00 Reduced full membership rate for those in receipt of unemployment assistance.
Senior €75.00 Reduced full membership rate for those aged 65 and over.
