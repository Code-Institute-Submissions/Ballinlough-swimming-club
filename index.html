Membership

Ballinlough Swimming Club is Ireland's newest swimming club at just 6 months old. We are always looking for new members. Although we are run entirely by volunteers, we incur expenses such as insurance and equipment costs.

South Sea Swim

In conjuntion with three other clubs we are planning the inaugural, "South Sea Swim". A challenging 5km swim from Cobh to Spike Island and back again.

Non-members allowed to participate but members have first preference. Wetsuits are highly recommended!
