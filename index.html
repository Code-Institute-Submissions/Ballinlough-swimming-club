Membership

Ballinlough Swimming Club is Ireland's newest swimming club at just 6 months old. We were founded by experienced members who wished to create a local club.

We are always looking for new members. Our members are of all swimming abilities and disciplines.

Although we are run entirely by volunteers, we incur expenses such as insurance and equipment costs.

  • Full
  • €100.00
  • Admittance to all training sessions, use of club equipment, subsidised club outings and entry to some competitions.
  • Student
  • €80.00
  • Reduced full membership rate for all full-time third level students.
  • Unemployed
  • €75.00
  • Reduced full membership rate for those in receipt of unemployment assistance.
  • Senior
  • €75.00
  • Reduced full membership rate for those aged 65 and over.
